Important Notes:

  • Always check expiry dates before using any medicine.
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms persist for more than 3 days.
  • Dosages mentioned are general guidance. Consult a doctor for infants, pregnant women, or chronic illness patients.
  • Store medicines in a cool, dry place, away from children.
